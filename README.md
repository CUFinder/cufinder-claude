# CUFinder Claude Desktop Extension

<img src="logo.png" alt="CUFinder Logo" width="200">

## Overview

**CUFinder** is a Claude Desktop extension that provides access to comprehensive B2B lead generation and data enrichment capabilities directly within your AI workflow. This extension leverages CUFinder's extensive database of over 1 billion enriched professional profiles and 85 million company records, delivering real-time business intelligence and contact discovery features exclusively through Claude Desktop.

- **Business Intelligence**: Access detailed company information including firmographics, funding data, and technology stack
- **Contact Discovery**: Locate and enrich business contacts with verified email addresses and phone numbers
- **Advanced Search**: Filter and discover businesses and professionals using 30+ targeted criteria
- **Real-Time Enrichment**: Retrieve up-to-date business and contact information refreshed daily
- **Seamless Integration**: Works natively within Claude Desktop environment

## Features

### Core Capabilities

- **Find Business**: Retrieve comprehensive information about any business using company name, domain, or LinkedIn URL
- **Find Person**: Access detailed professional profiles by searching with full name and company information
- **Search Businesses**: Discover companies using advanced filters including location, industry, employee size, founding year, funding data, revenue, products/services, and technology stack
- **Search Persons**: Locate professionals based on multiple criteria including name, job title, company details, location, industry, and influence level
- **Search Local Businesses**: Find local businesses within specific geographic areas using business name, location (country, state, city), and industry filters

### Data Access

## Installation & Configuration

This extension is intended for use **only within Claude Desktop**. No manual installation, configuration, or server setup is required.

- The extension is automatically loaded and managed by Claude Desktop
- All data access and integration are handled securely within the Claude Desktop environment
- Authentication and API access are managed through your CUFinder account credentials
- Data retrieval operates within CUFinder's credit-based pricing system

## Technical Specifications

### Search Filters & Parameters

**Business Search Filters:**
- Company name, domain, or LinkedIn URL
- Location (country, state, city)
- Industry classification (480+ industries)
- Company size (1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 201-500, 501-1000, 1001-5000, 5001-10000, 10001+)
- Revenue ranges (<$1M to >$1B)
- Funding round types (Seed, Angel, Series A-F, Private Equity, etc.)
- Technology stack detection
- Products and services keywords
- Investment status

**Contact Search Filters:**
- Full name
- Job title and job title categories (C-Suite, Engineering, Sales, Marketing, etc.)
- Company information
- Location (country, state, city)
- Industry
- Company employee size
- Revenue ranges
- Influence level (Niche, Emerging, Established, Prominent, Influential, Celebrity)
- Technology usage

**Local Business Filters:**
- Business name
- Geographic location with radius selection
- Country, state, and city
- Industry type (5000+ local business categories)

**Contact Data Includes:**
